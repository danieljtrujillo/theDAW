# theDAW Wiki

theDAW is an all-in-one music creation application by [GANTASMO](https://gantasmo.com). It generates audio from text prompts, init audio, painted inpaint regions, and the Chimera fusion engine, then opens into a studio for composition, arrangement, editing, and mixing, and into a live rig for DJing and VJing with deep MIDI mapping. It pairs with [theDAW-XR](https://github.com/gantasmo/theDAW-XR) on Meta Quest 3 and ships the first non-Mac port of Magenta RealTime 2.

This wiki orients and links into the canonical [User Guide](https://github.com/gantasmo/theDAW/blob/main/docs/USER_GUIDE.md), which holds the depth.

## GANTASMO

GANTASMO is an amorphous entity by [Daniel Joaquin Trujillo](https://github.com/danieljtrujillo) and [Josh Valenzuela](https://github.com/StarskreamEXE). It makes thought-provoking, highly technical, listenable music, and runs a research and development practice across artificial intelligence, augmented and virtual reality, the democratization of musical tools and education, and the preservation of musical history. Listen on [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/artist/4q5n0QgK6mvyuw8FRzhuNA), follow [Instagram](https://instagram.com/gantasmo) and [X](https://x.com/gantasmo), and learn more at [gantasmo.com](https://gantasmo.com).
